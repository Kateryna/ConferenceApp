About

VLDB is an annual conference held by the non-profit Very Large Data Base Endowment Inc.. The mission of VLDB is to promote and exchange scholarly work in databases and related fields throughout the world. The VLDB conference began in 1975 and is closely associated with SIGMOD and SIGKDD. Acceptance rate of VLDB, averaged from 1993 to 2007, is 16%[1] and the rate for the Core Database Technology track in VLDB 2009 is 16.7%[2].
